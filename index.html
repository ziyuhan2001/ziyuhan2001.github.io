Optimization of Aircraft Boarding Efficiency through Simulation Modeling

Developed in R, the Aircraft Boarding simulation model compares average boarding times for an Airbus A321 across Random, Back-to-Front, and WILMA processes. The simulation validated that the WILMA process delivers the fastest average boarding time of 13.88 minutes, significantly enhancing efficiency and reducing aircraft turnaround time.

The Aircraft Delay prediction model leverages OLS, AIC/BIC, and Shrinkage Methods to forecast delay times for U.S. domestic flights.
